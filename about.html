Hi, I'm Chris

I'm a video editor, filmmaker, and content creator based in Los Angeles, and I genuinely love what I do!

One of my favorite things about this work is where it takes you. Freelancing has pulled me into worlds I never expected: traveling internationally, working with renowned restaurants, covering news, building YouTube channels, and creating social campaigns for brands. Every project is a chance to learn something new, and I am so grateful for the experience I've gained from project to project.

From working in post to running full shoots on location, I'm most at home when I'm involved in the whole creative process. Whether I'm building out a social strategy for a brand launch or cutting a long-form series from scratch, I bring the same thing every time: show up prepared, work hard, and make something worth watching.

That same curiosity led me into a wide range of projects, and that experience has made me adaptable. No matter what space or team, I can jump right in, get up to speed, and start contributing right away.

Camera to cut, pre to post, I'm ready to plug in wherever a project needs me. I hope we get to work together soon!
